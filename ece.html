Welcome to Electronics and Communication
The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering(ECE) was established in the year 2003 with an intake of 60.With the approval of AICTE & JNTU the intake was increased from 60 to 120 in the academic year 2006-07. The Post Graduation courses: M.Tech in Embedded Systems was introduced in the academic year 2008-09 and M.Tech in DIGITAL ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS was introduced in the academic year 2009-10.The Department of ECE has also introduced a DIPLOMA course in Electronics and Communication Engineering in this academic year 2013-14.
The main objectives of the Department are: To produce graduates that are prepared for successful career in the areas associated with Electronics, Designing of Electronic and Communication Systems, and Microprocessors & Microcontroller based system designing;
To enable the students to apply their acquired knowledge in basic sciences & mathematics in solving Electronics & Communication Engineering problems;
To produce graduates that understand the overall human context in which electronics and Communication Technology activities take place;
