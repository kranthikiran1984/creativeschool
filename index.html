Engineering College OF Institute and Science

Engineering College OF Institute and Science(ECIS), established in the year 2003 by a group of educationists and philanthropists, is a pioneering engineering institution located in the municipal limits of Khammam town. ECIS is one of the very few Institutions permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH), Kukatpally, Hyderabad.ISO 9001 – 2008 for all the courses being offered.ECIS is accredited by lAO, USA (International Accreditation Organization). The colleges boasts of Digital library through SONET and NPTEL and access to IEEE journals is provided. Games & Sports and other extra-curricular activities are given priority for the over-all personality and development of the students. Technical workshops & seminars are being conducted regularly to give extra inputs to the students.
